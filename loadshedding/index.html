What will we do with your data

The Eskom loadshedding application collect the following basic profile information if you decided to make it available on your profile:

  • Profile picture
  • Email address
  • Name & Surname

The data collected from your profile is solely utilized to personalize and enhance your experience. This information enables us to tailor the service to your preferences, providing you with a more satisfying user experience. Rest assured that your data is treated with utmost confidentiality and is not shared with any third party. We prioritize the privacy and security of your information. Your trust is of paramount importance to us, and we are committed to safeguarding your privacy at all times.

Where is my data stored?

As part of our commitment to maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of your information, we store all user data in Microsoft Azure, a highly secure and reliable cloud computing platform. By storing data in Azure, we leverage the advanced security features and robust infrastructure provided by Microsoft. Azure adheres to industry-leading security standards and compliance frameworks, ensuring the confidentiality, availability, and integrity of your data.
